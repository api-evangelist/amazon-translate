Adobe Marketing Agent integration with Amazon Quick enables marketing teams to access campaign insights through natural language conversations. The solution shows how to configure MCP-based integration, authenticate with Adobe credentials, and retrieve marketing metrics including audience rankings, loyalty segment summaries, journey usage data, and conflict recommendations, with governance controls such as least privilege access, tenant isolation, audit logging, and human review. Implementation involves creating branded connector tiles, discovering MCP tools, registering them as actions, and using custom chat agents with approval workflows.
