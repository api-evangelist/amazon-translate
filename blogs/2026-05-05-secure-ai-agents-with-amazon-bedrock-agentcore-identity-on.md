AI agents in production require secure access to external services. Amazon Bedrock AgentCore Identity, available as a standalone service, secures how your AI agents access external services whether they run on compute platforms like Amazon ECS, Amazon EKS, AWS Lambda, or on-premises. An earlier post covered AgentCore Identity credential management for AI agents.
