Business leaders across industries rely on operational dashboards as the shared source of truth that their teams execute against daily. But dashboards are built to answer known questions. When teams need to explore further, ad-hoc, multi-dimensional, or unforeseen questions, they hit a bottleneck. They wait hours or days for BI teams to build new views or update reports. The Dataset Q&A feature bridges that gap. You can ask questions in natural language, get accurate answers in seconds, with no new dashboards to build, and no queue to wait in.
