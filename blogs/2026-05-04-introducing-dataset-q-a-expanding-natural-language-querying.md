Every BI team knows this bottleneck: a business user has a question that falls outside existing dashboards, so they file a ticket. An analyst writes the query, validates the results, and delivers them—hours or days later. Multiply that by hundreds of ad-hoc requests per month, and the backlog becomes the single biggest constraint on data team productivity. Amazon Quick now adds a powerful new natural language query capability, Dataset Q&A, to remove this bottleneck.
